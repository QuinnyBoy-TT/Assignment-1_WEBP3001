Amanti Del Cafe is a temporary small-scale business that provides a fairly vegetarian-friendly menu of short, savory sandwiches, pasta and sales choices. Prices are moderate and parking is available. They currently provide only for local customers, but the plan is to eventually expand for their goods and services in terms of e-commerce:


  • Aid in the future scalability of the business by expanding their reach.
  • To aid in making quick editions to the running of the business.
  • Provide a fully-fledged management system. This management provides a mean of managing:
    1. Customer Information
    2. Staff Information
    3. Online E-Commerce
    4. Inventory Management
  • Providing a point of data entry for relevant information pertaining to advertising and managing of prices.
  • Allow an avenue for customers to provide a testimonial and in depth (if applicable) on the quality of food and service provided.
